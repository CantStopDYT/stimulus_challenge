We're all hurting, but these organizations that bring so much value in service to our community have had to cancel fundraising events, close their doors or face related challenges. We want to do our part to support the places and faces of our community.

Stimulus checks from the federal government will be on their way to Americans soon. The #StimulusChallenge movement encourages people to give all or part of their stimulus check to a nonprofile and/or small business in their local community.

We will not be collecting any money, this is your promise to fulfill on your own elsewhere.
